Workflow

This page is written to prevent “surprise exclusions” and “we booked the wrong thing” problems. If you skip the consult, you’ll usually pay in delay.

1
Consult

Align scientific question, primary endpoint, constraints, and feasible paradigms.

2
Feasibility + design alignment

Confirm cohorts, run order, habituation needs, exclusions, and expected data outputs.

3
Schedule via iLab

Requests and approvals occur in iLab. Staff will confirm scope and logistics.

4
Run + QC + delivery

We run the study (or supervise limited self-service), document deviations, and deliver data.

Intake checklist (bring this)

  • Hypothesis and primary behavioral endpoint
  • Genotype/strain, age, sex, N per group (target)
  • Handling history and housing conditions
  • Any interventions (drug, surgery, viral, diet) with timing
  • Known constraints: light cycle, transport, phenotype severity

Common reasons studies fail

  • Underpowered design or shifting endpoints mid-run
  • Uncontrolled handling/stress differences
  • Ignoring light-cycle effects for circadian-sensitive tasks
  • Strain/vision limitations for spatial tasks
  • Booking equipment before defining the protocol
