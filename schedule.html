What to have ready

  • Primary endpoint and domains
  • Strain, age, sex, and handling history
  • Intervention timeline (drug/surgery/viral/diet) + constraints
  • Target window for testing + cohort availability
  • Full-service (default) vs self-service request (UNC only)

Before you submit

  • Define your primary endpoint and success criteria
  • Confirm cohorts and constraints (age/sex/strain, handling)
  • Choose the minimum set of assays that answer your question
  • Plan order effects, habituation, and washouts

After you submit

  • Staff review for feasibility and scheduling fit
  • Any required training/approvals identified
  • Run plan finalized (order, exclusions, outputs)
  • Scheduling confirmed and execution begins

Self-service requests (UNC only)

If you request self-service access, expect training requirements and a proficiency check. Some paradigms may remain staff-run due to complexity, safety, or QC requirements.
