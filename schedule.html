Before you submit a request

  • Define your primary endpoint and domains
  • Confirm cohorts and constraints (age/sex/strain, handling)
  • Know your intervention timeline (drug/surgery/viral/diet)
  • Decide full-service vs requesting self-service eligibility

After you submit

  • Staff review for feasibility and scheduling fit
  • Any required training/approvals are identified
  • Run plan finalized (order, exclusions, outputs)
  • Scheduling confirmed and execution begins

Self-service requests (UNC only)
